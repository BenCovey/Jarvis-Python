About Me

This is what I do.

My name is Benjamin Covey, I am a software developer who will be graduating from the NSCC-IT (Nova Scotia Community College) program spring 2017.

I have been interested in programming since I was young, I used to wonder how things worked. Everything from traffic lights to check out machines. That was where programming came into my life, I then took a high school course on programming. I learned Visual Basic, and built basic programs that looking back at are foolishly simple.

Since I started programming I have been constantly working on projects outside of class. From my first, an unencrypted password manager that required a password, to a fully functional voice recognition assistant program. I enjoy programming; I sit down at my desk and can code for hours without break. I honestly cannot imagine a better career to find myself in then something like programming

Recently I found myself interested in robotics, mostly in trying to build a two-legged robot that can maintain balance. I like the challenge and physical aspect behind hardware, and the complexity of logic in programming robotics.
